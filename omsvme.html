OpenMower Editor Controls:
🖱️ Move Points: Drag the blue points (area) or the red point (docking station).
🧲 Lock/Unlock Point: Hold ALT + Click on a point to lock it (Gold Square). Locked points are safe from deletion and simplification.
➕ Add Point: Double Click near a line to insert a new manual point.
➖ Remove Point: Right Click on a blue point to delete it.
🧭 Change Heading: Hold SHIFT + Drag the red dock to rotate its heading (green line).
🔍 View: Scroll Wheel to zoom, Drag empty space to pan the map.
